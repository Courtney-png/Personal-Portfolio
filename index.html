About Me:

Hi, my name is Courtney and I'm from England. I have a strong passion for both playing and making video games and love everything about it from conceptualisation to 3D Modelling,
to Programming and Refining. I've known what I wanted to go in for a lot of my life and have had enjoyed worldbuilding since I was very young, often planning out elaborate stories
with many characters and adventures for them to go on. In college, I finally started combining this with my love for games and would start writing outlines of my own potential games.
