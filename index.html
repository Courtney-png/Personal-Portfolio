About Me:

Hi, my name is Courtney and I'm from England. I have a strong passion for both playing and making video games and love everything about it from
conceptualisation to 3D Modelling, to Programming and Refining. I've known what I wanted to do for a lot of my life and have had enjoyed
worldbuilding since I was very young, often planning out elaborate stories with many characters and adventures for them to go on. In college,
I finally started combining this with my love for games and would start writing outlines of my own potential games.Though I still have a long
way to go in the games industry, creating a game from nothing is truly an amazing experience and I would be so grateful working on a team
with like-minded people.

My Current Projects:

Shiver

Shiver is my third year group project in university. It's a 3D sci-fi horror game with a focus on keeping yourself warm whilst also being cautious of the unknown threat potentially around any corner.

Adaption

A 2D creature collecting game where you must strategise to defeat other varieties whilst trying to figure out what has caused the current downfall of the world. With crafting, fighting and exploration, this is my current independent focus.

My long term project. A lot of the inital planning has been done for it such as a Game Design Document as well as feedback and the core concepts iterated upon. The main focus for it is a 2D action adventure game with the priority being the boss fights and how they feel.
